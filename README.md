## Key Insights Summary
- Strong demand for everyday products (Office Supplies)  
- High-value items (Electronics, Furniture) are major revenue contributors  
- Debit Card is the most profitable payment method  
- California & New York should remain marketing priority regions  
- Predictable seasonal peaks offer opportunities for better forecasting

## Business Recommendations
- Increase digital payment adoption through cashback/loyalty incentives  
- Strengthen inventory planning around peak seasons  
- Boost early-year sales with targeted promotional campaigns  
- Expand market reach in underperforming states  
- Build dashboards or forecasting models for continuous tracking
